About EU-Metal BV

EU-Metal BV is a blue chip company, operating as a Supplier and Exporter of a wide range of products. We are long engrossed in this business and try hard to make available Primary and Secondary stocks comprising of Hot Rolled Coils, Hot Dipped Galvanized Coils, Cold Rolled Coils, PPGI & PPGL Coils, Aluzinc Coils, Tin Free Coils, Tin Mill Black Plate Coils etc.  Working with a team of deft professionals, we have spread our business to the markets of Japan, South Korea, Indonesia, Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Sri Lanka, India, Middle East, All European countries, UK, Turkey, Egypt, and South African countries.
