COLD ROLLED GRAIN ORIENTED (CRGO) COILS

The CRGO Steel grains are much bigger than the regular steel. CRGO is usually supplied by the producing mills in coil form and has to be cut into “laminations”, which are then used to form a transformer core, which is an integral part of any transformer where its use is to reduce the core losses.

  • THICKNESS : 0.18-0.35 mm
  • WIDTH : 914 & 1000 mm
  • QUALITY : PRIME / NON-PRIME

PRODUCT RANGE

  • CRGO Grades: M3, M4, M5 & M6​
  • Hi-B and Laser grade materials are also available
